Max Temperature

The Latitudes vs Temperatures visualization is consistent with cooler temperatures in the highest elevation of the Northern and Southern Hemispheres.

These bands lay above +20 and below -20 of the Equator.

It is winter in the Southern Hemisphere (cooler) and summer (warmer) in the Northern Hemisphere per the 8/5/2018 plot. As you get close to the Equator, the temperatures are up. Cities and communities near the Equator always have warm temperatures, (except if they are in higher elevations). They are tropical by nature, overcast with rain (unless the Trade Winds are blowing).
