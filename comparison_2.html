Humidity

The Latitudes vs Humidity visualizations reveals a high number of cities in both the Northern and Southern Hemispheres at or near 100% humidity.

The data on humidity affects all latitudes and are not consentrated above +20 degrees and below -20 degrees of the Equator.

It is important to note, there appears to be a similar number of cities that have very low humidity. When you review the data you will find cities in well known arid locations. As you get close to the Equator, the temperatures are up. Humidity is related to rain or precipitation. Light winds and cloudiness will likey affect the humidity.
