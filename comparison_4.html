Wind Speed

The Latitudes vs Windspeed visualization is consistent with gentle summer winds in both the Southern and Northern Hemispheres.

The World experiences winds due to changes in atmospheric temperatures and pressures. Additionally, the rotation of the Earth provides constant movement.

Windspeed is a major component is weather. If we did not have Trade Winds, we would heat up and our equatorial regions would be unbearable. The change in wind speed and direction brings moisture and clouds. When these clouds move over land or bump into a different moving air mass, we experience severe and even violent weather. Track the Jet Stream and monitor how movements in air pressure cause weather or even stagnate weather patterns.
