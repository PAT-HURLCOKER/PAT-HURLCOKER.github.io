Humidity


Humidity is a weather phenomum that can make heat seem oppresive. Monsoon season is felt throughout the equatorial regions with the most favorable weather in late October through January. The humidity can feel so thick and heavy you might expect rain at any time.


The Latitudes vs Temperatures is consistent with cooler temperatures in the highest parts of the Northern and Southern Hemispheres. It is winter in the Southern Hemisphere (cooler) and summer in the Northern Hemisphere. As you get close to the Equator, the temperatures are up. The cities and communities near the Equator are always have warm temperatures (except if they are in higher elevations), tropical by nature, overcast with rain (unless the Trade Winds are blowing). These bands always are just above +20 and below -20 of the Equator.
